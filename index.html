There is often a body elected by the group to help deliver on decisions made by the community, but unlike a traditional Corporation the central leadership is not all powerful. Decisions get made from the bottom-up, governed by a community organized around a specific set of rules enforced on a blockchain. DAOs are internet-native organizations collectively owned and managed by their members. They have built-in treasuries that are only accessible with the approval of their members.
